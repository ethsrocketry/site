Our Mission

// 01

We turn curiosity into launch vehicles. ETHS Rocketry brings together students passionate about engineering, aerospace, and innovation to design, build, and launch high-power rockets. Our mission is to create opportunities for students to take on real engineering challenges, push their technical limits, and reach for goals that extend far beyond the classroom.

We are a subset of the Architecture, Engineering, and Trades Club.

Hands-On Rocketry

Build and fly real rockets. Every member gets their hands on the hardware.

Real Engineering Skills

Avionics, control systems, CAD, simulation, presentation — skills that carry into college and beyond.

National Competitions

Competing in ARC and R4S gives us measurable targets and deadlines that keep us focused.
